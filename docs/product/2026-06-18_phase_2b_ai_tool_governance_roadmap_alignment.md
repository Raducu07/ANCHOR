## 3. Future Phase 2B scope boundary

If and when the founder promotes this work (which is **future Phase 2B planning** only):

- ANCHOR's future role would be **practice-side question structuring** and **evidence-gap governance**.
- ANCHOR may help practices record: what AI tools are in use; what providers disclose; what is missing or unclear; who reviewed the information; what governance questions were asked; and when the review should be revisited.
- ANCHOR remains **structurer, not arbiter**.
- ANCHOR does **not** rate, validate, certify, accredit, recommend, approve, endorse, or clinically assess third-party AI tools.
- ANCHOR does **not** determine GDPR lawful basis, consent requirements, special-category-data treatment, or clinical suitability. Consent and data-movement prompts are framed as **"questions for your DPO / solicitor / provider"**, not ANCHOR conclusions.
