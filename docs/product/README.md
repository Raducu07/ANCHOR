# ANCHOR Product Design Docs

Product-design and positioning notes for the ANCHOR backend.

These are **planning and design documents only**. They do not authorise any build, schedule any feature, or change current priorities. ANCHOR's active work remains 2A-D release-candidate hardening per the operative canon (see Doctrine pointers below). Nothing here grants compliance, certification, RCVS approval, regulator endorsement, or guaranteed protection, and nothing here frames ANCHOR as rating, approving, validating, certifying, recommending, accrediting, or endorsing third-party AI tools — ANCHOR is the **structurer, not the arbiter**.
