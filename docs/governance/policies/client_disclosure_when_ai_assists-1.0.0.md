# Client Disclosure When AI Assists

**Status:** Template stub v1.0.0

This ANCHOR template is a governance support artefact for clinic review and adaptation. It is not legal advice, regulatory approval, clinical guidance, or proof of compliance. Adopting clinics remain responsible for the final policy that applies to their practice.

## Purpose

This template provides a starting point for transparent client communication when AI has assisted in producing materials such as discharge notes, written summaries, or other client-facing text.

## Principles (to adapt)

- Clients should be able to understand, at a level appropriate to the situation, when AI has contributed to material the clinic provides to them.
- The clinic remains accountable for the final content. AI is an assist, not a source of authority.
- Disclosure wording should be plain-language and not imply that AI guarantees accuracy or compliance.

## Scope (to adapt)

- Which client-facing materials are in scope.
- How and when disclosure is offered.
- Who in the clinic is responsible for the disclosure language.

## Out of scope

This template does not cover clinical decision-making and does not constitute regulatory communication guidance.

## Notes

Template content is to be developed and reviewed by the clinic before external use. ANCHOR does not certify, approve, or endorse any policy adopted from this template.
