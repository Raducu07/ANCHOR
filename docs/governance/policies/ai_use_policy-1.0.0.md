# AI Use Policy for Veterinary Teams

**Status:** Template stub v1.0.0

This ANCHOR template is a governance support artefact for clinic review and adaptation. It is not legal advice, regulatory approval, clinical guidance, or proof of compliance. Adopting clinics remain responsible for the final policy that applies to their practice.

## Purpose

This template provides a practice-wide starting point for describing how AI may be used in clinic workflows, the boundaries of acceptable use, and where human review is required. Adopting clinics should adapt the content to their own context before relying on it.

## Scope (to adapt)

- Which AI tools are in scope for this policy.
- Which clinic roles the policy applies to.
- Which workflows AI may assist with, and which it may not.

## Principles (to adapt)

- Human review remains required for AI-assisted outputs before clinical or client-facing use.
- AI is not a substitute for professional veterinary judgement.
- Staff record concerns or unexpected behaviour through the clinic's near-miss process.

## Out of scope

This template does not cover clinical decision-making, diagnostic reasoning, dosing, prescribing, or autonomous triage. AI tools used under this policy must not be relied on for those purposes.

## Notes

Template content is to be developed and reviewed by the clinic before external use. ANCHOR does not certify, approve, or endorse any policy adopted from this template.
