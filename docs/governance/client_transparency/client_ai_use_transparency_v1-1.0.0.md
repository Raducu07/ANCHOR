# Client AI Use Transparency Statement

**Status:** Template stub v1.0.0

This template is a governance support artefact for clinic review and adaptation. It is not legal advice, regulatory approval, a consent form, or a clinical record. Adopting clinics remain responsible for the final wording shown to their clients.

## Purpose

A plain-language explanation of bounded, human-reviewed AI use in the clinic. This statement helps the practice describe to clients how AI may be used in clinic workflows, what AI is not used for, and where human professional review remains required.

## What AI may be used for

AI may be used for drafting client communications from clinician-confirmed facts, internal summarisation, administrative support, and governance and learning support.

## What AI is not used for

AI is not used for diagnosis, prescribing, treatment planning, replacing veterinary judgement, or autonomous clinical decisions.

## Human review

The veterinary team remains responsible for clinical decisions and client-facing material. AI-assisted material is reviewed by a member of the veterinary team before it is used.

## Privacy and confidentiality

The clinic avoids entering unnecessary personal, client, or patient details into AI tools. The clinic remains responsible for data protection and client communication. Governance records about AI use are metadata-only.

## Questions from clients

Clients may ask the clinic how AI was used in any specific situation. Clinic staff should explain clearly and in plain language.

## Adaptation notes

This template is to be reviewed and adapted by the clinic before being shown to clients. ANCHOR does not certify, approve, or endorse any statement adopted from this template.
